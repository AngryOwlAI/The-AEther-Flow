Implement the Director role-flexibility policy. Registered roles remain stable
templates and guidance. The Director may bind a one-job execution context using
direct registered-role use, task overlays, or one-job provisional roles. Task
overlays must preserve the registered-role identity while recording the
task-specific authority delta. Provisional roles may be brand-new one-job roles
or template-derived roles with distinct temporary identities. Protected
authority expansion remains human-gated: claim promotion, canonical ontology
authority, benchmark-status authority, Gate Chair authority, or permanent role
registration. Recurring provisional-role patterns must surface a
project-system signal after three observed uses so human-authorized permanent
