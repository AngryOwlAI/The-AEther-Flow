High-Level Model
Dual mission
The project is both a physics research program and an AI research-agent development program.
Physics center
The physics track studies whether exact ordinary GR can be interpreted and eventually derived from the Æther-flow ontology.
AI system center
The AI track builds a human-accountable research system for theoretical physics through bounded roles, validation, memory, and claim gates.
Open result
The first-principles derivation is not claimed. The hub keeps that uncertainty visible.
