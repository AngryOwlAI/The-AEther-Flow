What This Explainer Describes
What it is
Role routing is the project's decision system for assigning bounded work to the correct registered role and preserving the reason for that assignment.
Role or function
It connects task state, Director decisions, role contracts, execution overlays, and registries so agents know who owns a change, what is allowed, what evidence is required, and what must stop.
Why it matters first
Read this first because the diagrams and tables are about authority boundaries, not personnel labels. Correct routing prevents physics claims, documentation edits, validator work, and memory refreshes from collapsing into one vague workflow.
Source basis
The summary is grounded in research-control guidance, role and execution registries, Director decision records, and the execution-role schema that defines role overlays and task-local authority. The chips point to the durable records behind each routing decision.

Analysis capsules
Claim-Aware Analysis
Role Selection Is Authority Selection
Premise
Choosing a role is choosing the authority boundary for one bounded task.
Mechanism
The Director compares the task against registered role contracts, source classes, allowed writes, forbidden surfaces, validators, and claim boundaries before opening an AgentJob.
Source Basis
`research_control/README.md`, `registries/AGENT_ROLE_REGISTRY.csv`, and `registries/DIRECTOR_DECISION_REGISTRY.csv`.
Authority Status
Workflow explanation; it does not alter role authority.
Uncertainty
A role template may be close but not exact; the execution-role record is the job-specific contract.
Validation Or Test
Check the Director decision, execution-role record, and AgentJob allowlist.
Next Step
Inspect the task-local execution-role file before executing or reviewing work.
Execution Role Prevents Drift
Premise
The execution-role record prevents a role from silently gaining or losing authority during a task.
Mechanism
It records whether the job uses a registered role, task overlay, or one-job provisional role, then binds added constraints, removed permissions, expanded permissions, allowed writes, human gates, and expiry.
Source Basis
`registries/ROLE_EXECUTION_REGISTRY.csv` and `.agents/schemas/EXECUTION_ROLE_SCHEMA.md`.
Authority Status
Project-control explanation.
Uncertainty
Repeated provisional patterns may indicate a role-design gap, but they do not become permanent by convention.
Validation Or Test
Validate role execution rows against task-local role records and AgentJob fields.
Next Step
Use the research-system explainer to see how the selected role executes inside the full task loop.
