Three-layer model
High Level, Operational, Evidence
High-Level Model
Æther
A proposed deeper four-dimensional substrate of reality.
Æther-flow
The intrinsic ordered motion or relational organization of that substrate.
Observed space
A local experiential slice through which embedded observers encounter the deeper substrate.
S-time
The experienced order of change arising from matter, light, and Æther-flow.
Gravity
Heuristically interpreted as mass-shaped reorganization of surrounding Æther-flow.
Benchmark
Observable-scale physics remains ordinary exact GR until a derivation gate is passed.
Operational Model
Ontology frames the target
It says what the project takes the deeper structure to be about.
Benchmark constrains the target
The observable package remains one operative Lorentzian metric, universal matter coupling, clocks, causal structure, and standard empirical content.
Derivation carries the burden
A future candidate must recover the benchmark from source-defined substrate data without target import.
Gates prevent overclaiming
Compatibility or adoption is not the same as a first-principles derivation.

Project Specific Ontology
Premise
The Æther-flow ontology is the project-specific substrate and flow model.
Mechanism
It links Æther, Æther-flow, observed space, S-time, expansion, and gravity while keeping exact GR as the operational benchmark.
Source Basis
ontology/aether-and-aether-flow.md, README.md, and CLAIM_BOUNDARY_REGISTRY.csv.
Authority Status
Human-only conceptual explanation; registered TeX remains scientific authority.
Uncertainty
The ontology has not yet supplied a source theorem that derives the benchmark.
Validation Or Test
A candidate must source-construct observer readout and recover Lorentzian geometry, clocks, matter coupling, invariance, and closure without smuggling.
Next Step
Inspect the derivation-burden map before treating a candidate as progress.
Benchmark Adoption
Premise
Exact GR is adopted as the observable benchmark while derivation remains open.
Mechanism
The benchmark is a constraint on candidate work, not evidence that the ontology has succeeded.
Source Basis
README.md, AGENTS.md, and TEX_SOURCE_REGISTRY.csv.
Authority Status
Explanatory summary of registered scientific boundary status.
Uncertainty
No source-defined substrate law has yet been accepted as deriving the benchmark.
Validation Or Test
Check anti-smuggling, invariance, dimensional consistency, matter coupling, and known limiting behavior.
Next Step
Route candidate mechanisms through bounded research-control jobs and claim gates.
