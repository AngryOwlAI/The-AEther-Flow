Three-layer model
High Level, Operational, Evidence
High-Level Model
Purpose
Make theoretical work auditable instead of conversationally drifting.
Director
Selects one bounded role/job boundary from tracked state.
AgentJob
Binds objective, reads, writes, outputs, validators, and claim boundary.
Receipts
Completions, registries, and handoffs preserve the result and next state.
Operational Model
Resolve state
Start from tracked task state, latest handoff, and registries.
Open one job
The Director creates at most one bounded AgentJob per invocation.
Execute role
The selected execution role works only inside its allowlist and constraints.
Validate and hand off
Validators, completion records, and handoffs decide what can safely continue.

Analysis Capsules
Each capsule uses the required source-backed schema.
Bounded Transaction
Premise
The research system advances through bounded transactions, not open-ended edits.
Mechanism
Director decisions create AgentJobs, role contracts constrain execution, and validators check outputs before handoff.
Source Basis
research_control/README.md, research_control/AGENTS.md, and control registries.
Authority Status
Project-control explanation, not scientific proof.
Uncertainty
A completed transaction may record progress, obstruction, repair, or negative result.
Validation Or Test
Inspect task files, completion records, registry rows, command receipts, and diff boundaries.
Next Step
Use role routing to understand why a specific execution role was selected.
Two Modes
Premise
Physics continuation and project-system improvement are separate modes.
Mechanism
continue-research handles physics state; improve-project-system handles roles, validators, docs, and memory machinery.
Source Basis
continue-research and improve-project-system skills plus AGENTS.md.
Authority Status
Workflow explanation only.
Uncertainty
Improving the system can improve reliability without changing scientific status.
Validation Or Test
Check skill, role, AgentJob, and claim-boundary rows for the selected mode.
Next Step
Keep workflow completion separate from claim acceptance.
