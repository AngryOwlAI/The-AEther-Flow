The Æther-flow Interpretation of Relativity
Consistency
Abstract
This paper states the consistency status of the adopted GR sector of Æther-flow Interpretation
of Relativity, the exact-closure theory of Æther / Æther-flow framework. The scope is deliberately
narrow. The manuscript does not claim a substrate-level proof of emergence from explicit
Æther variables. It analyzes only the adopted effective theory already fixed in the Æther-flow
Interpretation of Relativity dynamics paper: Einstein–Hilbert gravity with universal matter
coupling.
With that scope made explicit, the consistency result is also explicit. Around admissible
backgrounds, the linearized gravitational sector of Æther-flow Interpretation of Relativity has
the ordinary diffeomorphism gauge redundancy, the ordinary constraint structure, and the
ordinary two tensor propagating degrees of freedom of GR. At the effective level there is no extra
gravitational scalar sector, no extra vector sector, no independent preferred-frame propagating
field, no ghost kinetic term, and no tachyonic mass term introduced by the exact-closure theory
itself. Subject to the standard health assumptions on the adopted matter sector, Æther-flow
Interpretation of Relativity is therefore mathematically healthy at the effective level and can
serve as the reference and fallback theory of the wider Æther / Æther-flow program.
Within the flagship exact-closure package, the overview is the front door, the Exact-Closure
Note is the short anchor, and the present paper is the consistency module of the five-paper
modular full statement. Its role is to show that the adopted exact law is internally controlled
before the sequence turns to observer-facing relativistic recovery and flow geometry. That
ordering keeps exact closure first and derivational continuation secondary.

7. Consistency Status of Æther-flow Interpretation of Relativity
The manuscript can now state its main result directly. The chosen Æther-flow Interpretation of
Relativity formulation is internally controlled at the effective level. No hidden unresolved mode
issue is being buried in prose. The theory inherits the standard consistency of GR because the
theory is GR at the effective dynamical level, supplemented only by the deeper Æther / Æther-flow
ontology as interpretive structure.
The status statement should therefore be read in the following precise way.
1. The adopted exact-closure theory is a mathematically healthy effective gravitational theory.
2. Its propagating gravitational content is the ordinary massless spin-2 content of GR.
3. It is free of theory-specific ghost, tachyonic, gradient, extra-vector, and extra-scalar pathologies
at that level.
4. It remains the reference and fallback theory of the wider Æther / Æther-flow program.
8. What This Manuscript Does Not Establish
The present result has a strict boundary.
1. It does not provide a substrate-level degree-of-freedom count for explicit Æther variables.
2. It does not prove that Einsteinian exact closure has already emerged from a completed
microphysical action.
3. It does not analyze every possible exotic matter sector one could couple to GR; the consis-
tency statement assumes the standard healthy matter sector already adopted in Æther-flow
Interpretation of Relativity.
4. It does not replace the later relativistic-recovery manuscript, which should discuss observer-
level weak-field phenomenology, redshift, time dilation, light propagation, and the explicit SR
relation in fuller detail.
These limitations do not weaken the present result. They define it correctly. The purpose of this
manuscript is to establish whether the chosen exact-closure formulation is internally healthy. That
purpose can be fulfilled without pretending that the deeper derivational program is already finished.
9. Conclusion
This paper completes the consistency analysis of the adopted GR sector in the exact-closure reading
of Æther-flow Interpretation of Relativity. Once the theory is stated exactly as Einstein–Hilbert
gravity with universal matter coupling, its effective consistency structure is no longer ambiguous.

two. The quadratic theory carries no theory-specific ghost, no tachyonic graviton mass, no extra
gravitational scalar or vector mode, and no second universal low-energy causal cone.
